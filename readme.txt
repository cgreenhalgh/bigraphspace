An exploration/implementation of the Reactive XML realisation of bigraphs, as proposed and first developed by Thomas Hildebrandt, Jacob Winther (ITU MSc-thesis, 2004), Henning Niss and Martin Olsen at the The Concurrency and Mobility Group at the IT University of Copenhagen.
Has quickly evolved into a bigraph version of a tuple space a la EQUIP (object space).
